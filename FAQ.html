EduWatch is a digital platform designed to streamline and automate the attendance monitoring system for faculty members. It replaces traditional paper-based methods, providing a more efficient and transparent way to track faculty participation in academic events and institutional obligations.

The system is primarily for faculty members, professors, lecturers, and academic staff. It is also designed to be used by administrators to oversee and manage faculty records and data.

EduWatch ensures compliance with accreditation standards, simplifies reporting for professional development, and aids in data-driven decision-making for resource allocation. By providing verifiable records, it enhances transparency in governance and supports performance evaluations and promotion processes.
