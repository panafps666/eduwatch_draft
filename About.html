EduWatch - Faculty Attendance Monitoring System

This digital platform streamlines the management of faculty participation in academic events and institutional obligations by replacing inefficient paper-based tracking methods that often lead to incomplete records, administrative burden, and limited data analysis. Designed for faculty members, professors, lecturers, and academic staff, it supports accreditation requirements, ensures compliance with professional development mandates, and enables data-driven decision-making for resource allocation. By providing verifiable records of faculty involvement, the system enhances transparency in governance, aids in performance evaluations, promotion processes, and institutional accountability, while reducing manual administrative tasks. Additionally, its New Hire Registration feature simplifies onboarding by automatically creating user accounts, assigning role-based access permissions, and seamlessly integrating newly recruited personnel into the monitoring system.
